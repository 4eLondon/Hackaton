About PlantLink

Reimagining Agriculture for the Digital Age

PlantLink was founded to bridge the gap between traditional farming communities and modern digital tools. We believe every farmer deserves access to technology that was once only available to large agribusinesses.

Our platform empowers smallholder farmers across Jamaica and the Caribbean to sell produce directly to buyers, access AI-driven crop guidance, and make weather-informed decisions that protect their harvests and livelihoods.

Empower Farmers

Give smallholder farmers the tools to compete in the modern economy.

Connect Communities

Link buyers directly to verified local farmers, cutting costs for everyone.

Drive Innovation

Bring AI, data, and smart tech into everyday agricultural decisions.

Why Smart Agriculture Matters

The Caribbean loses an estimated $400M+ in agricultural value annually due to poor market access, weather damage, and outdated practices. PlantLink addresses this with data, connectivity, and AI.

  • ✅ Reduces post-harvest losses
  • ✅ Improves market access for small farms
  • ✅ Enables climate-smart farming decisions
  • ✅ Builds food security region-wide
